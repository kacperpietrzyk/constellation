# Support

Constellation is pre-alpha and does not yet provide end-user support or a
stable release.

- Use [Discussions](https://github.com/kacperpietrzyk/constellation/discussions)
  for questions, product ideas, and early design feedback.
- Use [Issues](https://github.com/kacperpietrzyk/constellation/issues) for
  reproducible bugs and scoped, actionable proposals.
- Use [private vulnerability reporting](SECURITY.md) for security concerns.

Please do not include private meeting content, calendar data, credentials, or
other personal information in public reports.
